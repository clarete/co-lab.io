About

Masaryk Co-Lab creates processes to make community work more effective through leveraging digital technology.

We go where technology can be helpful for people that are getting their hands dirty and working with community. Currently we're partnering with Masaryk Towers, a housing community in the Lower East Side, to develop a model of tech based community work, including a computer lab and health management tools.
